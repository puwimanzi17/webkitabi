2

Vision

We are a youth social enterprise comprised of local people dedicated to advocate for biodiversity conservation, ecotourism promotion, community health, environmental management, and climate change mitigation and adaptation. The organization is working to enhance community awareness on biodiversity conservation within the five districts that surround the Nyungwe National Forest: Rusizi, Nyamasheke, Karongi, Nyamagabe and Nyaruguru.

Mission

We are aiming to sustain our projects by promoting ecotourism of our products by Integrating biodiversity conservation and enhancing community awareness.
